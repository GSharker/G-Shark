Struct ConvexHull.Face

Struct representing a single face.

Vertex0, Vertex1 and Vertex2 are the vertices in CCW order. They acutal points are stored in the points array, these are just indexes into that array.

Opposite0, Opposite1 and Opposite2 are the keys to the faces which share an edge with this face. Opposite0 is the face opposite Vertex0 (so it has an edge with Vertex2 and Vertex1), etc.

Normal is (unsurprisingly) the normal of the triangle.
